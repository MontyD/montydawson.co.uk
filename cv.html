Victoria Forms

Consultant

Victoria Forms is one of the leading providers of web forms for Local Government and large businesses. My time at Victoria Forms has allowed me to further my development skills, grow my confidence, and gain experience of working with customers in a web development environment. I quickly gained experience of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, eventually being tasked with managing the redevelopment of the majority of our customers' front end design. I gained experience of working with backend technologies as well - writing a custom webapp that automated key processes, setting up a number of 3rd party applications on Linux and Windows cloud servers, and helping to maintain the architecture of our main product.

I was tasked with managing entire projects on my own, offering ongoing support to the customer, managing time frames, providing and off-site training. Furthermore I was often required to work as part of a team - helping to organise a joint effort to meet the customer's needs.
